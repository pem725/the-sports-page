# Mets Buy-or-Sell Watch — 2026 Season

## The Editorial Question

With Clay Holmes on the IL (fractured fibula, weeks-to-months), the Mets' rotation is being held together by Nolan McLean and a thin depth chart behind him. The roster decision point is approaching: **will Mets management treat 2026 as a lost year and pull up minor leaguers for development reps, OR double down at the trade deadline to buy a championship-caliber pitching staff?** Both are defensible. Both have very different implications for 2027 and beyond. The eventual piece tracks the front office's actual choice against what the math says they should have done.

## Decision Points

- **All-Star Break**: Tuesday, July 14, 2026 — first opportunity for a public signal
- **Trade Deadline**: Friday, July 31, 2026 — the actual buy-or-sell line
- **September 1 roster expansion**: secondary signal of development priorities

## What to Track

For each starting pitcher in the projected rotation:
- IP, ERA, FIP, xFIP, SIERA
- Per-start basis where useful (e.g., catastrophic outings)

For the team:
- W-L trajectory; Pythagorean expected W-L; BaseRuns expected W-L
- Run differential weekly snapshot
- Distance from AL/NL Wild Card 3 line

For the front office signals:
- Minor-league call-ups (especially pitchers)
- Veteran rest / DH-only days suggesting protection
- Public statements from David Stearns
- Trade chatter (always grain of salt)
